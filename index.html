Understanding Common Conditions

A reference mapping visual guides to help recognize early diagnostic symptoms requiring intervention support.

Hearing Impairment

Difficulty tracing audio directions, asking for constant repetition, missing environmental sound signals, or speech clarity reduction.

Speech & Delay Challenges

Inability to construct age-appropriate sentences, difficulty forming complex speech sounds, or structural articulation hurdles.

Fluency/Stammering

Repetitions of initial sounds, long pauses while attempting word formulations, or physical struggle behaviors during speech blocks.
